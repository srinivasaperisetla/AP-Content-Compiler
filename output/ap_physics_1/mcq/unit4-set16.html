  • A. $0.05 \text{ s}$
  • B. $0.1 \text{ s}$
  • C. $0.1 \text{ s}$ (Impulse $J = \Delta p = m \Delta v = 0.2 \text{ kg} \times (0 - 30 \text{ m/s}) = -6 \text{ N} \cdot \text{s}$. The magnitude of force is $60 \text{ N}$. $J = F \Delta t$. $6 \text{ N} \cdot \text{s} = 60 \text{ N} \times \Delta t$. $\Delta t = 6/60 = 0.1 \text{ s}$.) Let's make sure options are correct.
  • D. $0.2 \text{ s}$
