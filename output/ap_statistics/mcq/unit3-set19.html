  • B. An observational study
  • C. A survey
  • D. A census

  • C. People with a predisposition to Parkinson's disease are less likely to drink coffee.

  • C. There is no relationship between sunscreen use and skin cancer.
  • D. There is an association between regular sunscreen use and lower skin cancer incidence, but causation cannot be inferred due to potential confounding variables.

  • C. It gives every adult male in the city an equal chance of being selected, leading to a representative sample and minimizing bias.
