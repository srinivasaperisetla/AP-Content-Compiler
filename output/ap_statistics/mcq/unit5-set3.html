Q1. (ID: ap_statistics_MCQ_U5S3Q1)
A polling organization takes two independent random samples of 500 likely voters to estimate the proportion who support Candidate A. The first sample yields $\hat{p}_1 = 0.53$, and the second yields $\hat{p}_2 = 0.56$. Which of the following is a valid statistical question suggested by these results?
  • A. Why did the two sample proportions differ?
  • B. What is the true proportion of voters who support Candidate A?
  • C. How many voters were in the population?
  • D. Is Candidate A likely to win the election?
