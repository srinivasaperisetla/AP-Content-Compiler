  • A. Yes, because both sample sizes are close to 30.
  • B. Yes, because the difference in proportions is 0.2.
  • C. No, because $n_1 p_1 = 20(0.8) = 16 \ge 10$ and $n_1 (1-p_1) = 20(0.2) = 4 < 10$.
