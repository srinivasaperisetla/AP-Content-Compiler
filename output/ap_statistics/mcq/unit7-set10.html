  • B. Since the p-value ($0.008$) is very small, there is no evidence to conclude that the true mean battery life is less than 12 hours.
  • C. Since the p-value ($0.008$) is less than $\alpha$ ($0.05$), we accept the alternative hypothesis that the mean battery life is less than 12 hours.
