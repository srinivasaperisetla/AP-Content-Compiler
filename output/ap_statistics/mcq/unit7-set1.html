They apply the new fertilizer to one-half of each of 20 randomly selected plots of land, and the standard fertilizer to the other half. The yield from both halves of each plot is recorded. Which inference procedure is most appropriate to analyze these data?

  • B. A two-sample t-interval for the difference between two population means.
  • C. A matched-pairs t-interval for the mean difference.
  • D. A two-sample z-interval for the difference between two population proportions.
