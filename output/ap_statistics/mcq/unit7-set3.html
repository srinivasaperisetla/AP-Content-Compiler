  • C. Since the p-value ($0.018$) is less than $\alpha$ ($0.05$), we reject $H_a$. There is not enough evidence to conclude the average IQ is higher than 100.
  • D. Since the sample mean (103) is greater than 100, we can conclude that the average IQ is higher than 100, regardless of the p-value.
