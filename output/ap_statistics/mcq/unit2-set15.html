Which of the following best describes the relationship between study hours and exam scores?

  • B. A strong, positive, linear association with no outliers.
  • C. A strong, positive, curved association with no outliers.
  • D. A weak, positive, linear association with an outlier.
Q2. (ID: ap_statistics_MCQ_U2S15Q2)

  • C. Approximately 78% of the variation in resting heart rate is explained by the number of exercises per week.
  • D. For every additional exercise per week, the resting heart rate decreases by 0.78 beats per minute.
