Q1. (ID: U7-Q1-INT)

The resulting interval was (28.5 cm, 34.1 cm). Which of the following is a correct interpretation of the confidence interval?
  • A. There is a 95% probability that the sample mean length is between 28.5 cm and 34.1 cm.
  • B. There is a 95% probability that a randomly selected fish will have a length between 28.5 cm and 34.1 cm.

  • D. If many random samples of size 25 are taken from this lake, about 95% of the sample means will be between 28.5 cm and 34.1 cm.
